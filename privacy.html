SpinPin — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

The short version

SpinPin does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no logins, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers.

Data we collect

None. SpinPin does not collect any data about you or your device.

Data stored on your device

SpinPin saves your game progress (highest level reached, lifetime stats, and local leaderboard times) to your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device. Deleting the app removes it permanently.

Third-party services

SpinPin does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's privacy

SpinPin does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
