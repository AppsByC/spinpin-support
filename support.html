How to play

Tap the screen to launch a pin onto the spinning disc. Don't hit the pins already there. Clear the level's pin count to advance.

Troubleshooting

Game won't launch or crashes: Force-quit the app and reopen. If it persists, restart your device or reinstall.

Progress reset: Progress is stored locally on your device. Reinstalling or clearing app data will reset levels and stats.

Sound issues: Check the silent switch and your device volume.

Privacy

SpinPin does not collect personal data, does not track you, and does not require an account. Game progress is stored only on your device.
